New Year new logo

The past year has flown by here at kiwipace and I’m delighted to say that things have been hectic and busy! I was able to develop a pakage tracking app for the Pebble watch and led the work on a client’s new web app.

The Pebble app came out in the summer under the name ‘Stamp’ and the other app is still in development. Here’s an app-store link for Stamp where you can see the new logo I just uploaded link. Combined with my full time job time has been the biggest antagonist, but it’s all worked out.

I have a great feeling that this upcoming year is going to rock for us and I have several ideas that we will most likely be working on in the coming weeks. Here’ to a great 2017 and some awesum coding to come!

Update on the Pebble story.

Word on the street is that fitbit purchased Pebble, bringing the Pebble watch production to an end. This was definetly a bit of a bummer, but I really enjoyed developing for the watch and thank Pebble for making the process fun and approachable. I will continue to keep stamp running for now, and keep my eyes peeld as to what fitbit has in mind.
